Explain : 

<a th:href="@{'/displayMessages/page/' + ${currentPage} + '?sortField=name&sortDir=' + ${reverseSortDir}}">Name</a> 

==> when they click on the "Name" it will go to the page : th:href="@{'/displayMessages/page/'
and it will provide the "${currentPage}"  

==> And if the user click on the arrow dot "'?sortField=name&sortDir=' + ${reverseSortDir}}" it will provide the sort on the name that they click.
