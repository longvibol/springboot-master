2. embed google map to website 

How to Get Your Own Embed Code for Any Location:
- Open Google Maps and search for your desired location.
- Click the Share button.
- Select Embed a map, then copy the iframe URL.
- Replace the existing src in your iframe with the new one.

We use <iframe>

Example :

<div class="map">
        <iframe
            src="https://www.google.com/maps/embed?pb=!1m18!1m12!1m3!1d31270.834276113543!2d104.86726899405161!3d11.562301979971156!2m3!1f0!2f0!3f0!3m2!1i1024!2i768!4f13.1!3m3!1m2!1s0x310951a354c389d9%3A0xd07843ea8349e8c1!2sRoyal%20Phnom%20Penh%20Hospital!5e0!3m2!1sen!2skh!4v1747558410141!5m2!1sen!2skh"

    </div>
